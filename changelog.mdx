## Stay Updated

Follow these steps to receive changelog notifications and apply updates to your workflows.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe to Alerts" icon="bell">
    Visit the [client portal](https://portal.hausauf-sand.com) and enable email notifications under Account > Preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes" icon="book-open">
    Check this changelog after each release and download updated templates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate Updates" icon="settings">
    Apply new methodologies to your next team session. Test `StabilityMetrics` with sample data:

    ```yaml
    organization:
      health_score: 85
      risks:
        - name: Market Volatility
          score: 7.2
        - name: Team Burnout
          score: 6.1
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
