## Step-by-Step Getting Started Process

Begin your journey with these sequential steps. Each phase builds on the previous to ensure a smooth transition to our support.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Make Initial Contact" icon="phone">
    Reach out via email, phone, or our contact form. Describe your challenge briefly, such as team conflicts or leadership gaps.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Include key details like company size and urgency to speed up our response.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule Free Consultation" icon="calendar">
    We reply within one business day to book a 30-minute call. Prepare to discuss your goals and current pain points.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Assessment" icon="clipboard-list">
    During the call, we evaluate your needs. Receive a customized proposal outlining services, timeline, and fees.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Onboard and Engage" icon="rocket">
    Sign the agreement and start with your first sparring session. Access our shared workspace for ongoing collaboration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
